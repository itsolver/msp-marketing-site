For getting the right support, choose your channel.

In addition to scheduled call-outs for hardware repairs, you have more immediate support options. Get your questions answered by an expert via phone, chat or email. From setting up your device to recovering your account to upgrading your laptop, IT Solver has you covered.

Chat

Get real-time assistance from an IT expert right there on your computer or mobile device.
Tap on the Help button in the bottom-right corner.

Phone

You can call us (07 3123 6000) immediately or at your convenience, or you can schedule a time for us to call you.

Email

For more straightforward questions — about an issue with software, for instance — send us an email and we’ll quickly reply with a solution.
Email: support@itsolver.net
